The Best Movies According to Mubashir

My top three movies of all time

3 ediots

Perfect combination of life lessons and laughter. Points out all the flaws of our education system in a very subtle but powerful way. Teaches us to strive for excellence and not for grades.

Intern

A perfect feel good movie about how a senior person starts his life again and helps a young CEO with his experience.

SpiderMan No Way Home

The three generations of spiderman teaming together to help all the old villains is a must watch. Perfect hit of nostalgia.
